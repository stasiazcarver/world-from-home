About

Holiday plans cancelled? Don't despair.

At World From Home, we believe virtual travel can be just as eye-opening.

We've made it easy for you to dive into the sights, sounds and tastes of different countries around the globe, through carefully curated food and culture recommendations.
Use our interactive map to choose your destination, or browse our A-Z guide of countries to see all our recommendations in one place. And join in by recommending your favourite films, music and recipes from countries missing from our map!

Ethical travel

World From Home was built primarily in response to the travel restrictions caused by the Covid-19 pandemic, but this isn't just a project for lockdown. The climate crisis demands that we fly less, consume less; we want to show that you don't have to go on a mini-break abroad to experience other cultures.

We also want this site to provide a cultural resource that will enrich and inspire the trips our readers do make, helping you discover cultural aspects of your destination you might not have known about, or even inspiring you to explore places you might not have otherwise visited!

Happy travelling!
